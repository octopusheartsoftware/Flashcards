Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Free Flashcard app ("the app") handles your information.

Summary

Free Flashcard does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All information you create in the app stays on your own device.

What information the app stores

The app stores the following directly on your device, using your browser's or device's local storage:

  • Deck names you create
  • Flashcard content you enter, including any text and images you add to cards
  • Study progress data (such as how many times a card should appear)
  • Your deck order and archive status

None of this information ever leaves your device. It is not sent to any server, and the developer of this app has no access to it.

Information we do not collect

The app does not collect, and has no access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data from your device's contacts, camera, or microphone

Images you add to cards

If you choose to add an image to a flashcard, that image is stored only on your device, in the same local storage as the rest of your deck data. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Data deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting decks within the app, or by clearing the app's storage/data through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this app's data practices change in the future (for example, if cloud sync or account features are added), this policy will be updated to reflect those changes before they take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out through the contact information listed on this app's Google Play Store page.
